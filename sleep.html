Sleep

Sleep is a vital aspect of our overall well-being. It's during sleep that our bodies repair, rejuvenate, and consolidate memories. Getting enough quality sleep is essential for both physical and mental health..

Why is Sleep Important?

  • Physical Health:


    Sleep helps regulate hormones, boosts the immune system, and aids in muscle repair

  • Mental Health:


    Sleep is crucial for memory consolidation, mood regulation, and cognitive function.

  • Chronic Disesase:


    Lack of sleep is linked to an increased risk of chronic conditions like heart disease, obesity, and diabetes.

How Much Sleep Do You Need?

  • Adults:


    Most adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night.

  • Teenagers:


    Teenagers require 8-10 hours of sleep.

  • Children:


    The amount of sleep needed varies by age, but generally, younger children require more sleep.
